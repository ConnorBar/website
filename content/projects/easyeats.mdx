---
title: "EasyEats"
---

The problem: you get home from work you're hungry, need to make dinner, not sure what you want nor what you even have ingredients for... bah! too much to think about and you end up just eating out instead. EasyEats is meant to fix that.

Track what's in your pantry, save recipes, and see exactly what you can cook right now with what you have. When you complete a recipe it auto-deducts the ingredients so your inventory stays accurate without manual updates.

Planned additions include grocery lists with per-store pricing, a drag-and-drop meal plan calendar, and smarter recipe import (paste a URL and it parses the ingredients automatically).

Built with Flutter for cross-platform mobile, FastAPI on the backend, and MongoDB for flexible schema on the recipe/ingredient data since recipes are wildly inconsistent in structure.

Potential for integration with E-Clair...

Still in progress.
