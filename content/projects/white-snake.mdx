---
title: "Legend of the White Snake"
---

白蛇传 (Legend of the White Snake) is one of the four great Chinese folk tales — a story about a white snake spirit who takes human form, falls in love, and gets entangled in a conflict between the mortal world and the spiritual one. The story has been retold hundreds of times across dynasties: as opera, film, TV drama, manga. Each retelling shifts the moral framing completely depending on the era it was made in.

This is my version: a 2D RPG with branching decisions and multiple endings that reflects that interpretive tradition. The idea is that you play through the story making choices that pull you toward different readings of it — is Bai Suzhen a villain or a victim? Is Xu Xian a coward or a man caught between worlds?

Built in Godot. Still early. Passion project that moves at whatever pace it moves at.
